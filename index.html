Research

My work connects nuclear physics, computational modeling, data analysis, and quantitative problem solving.

Gluon Saturation at the EIC

Studying gluon saturation through geometric scaling at the Electron-Ion Collider, with a novel focus on using a spatially dependent nuclear parton distribution function to study centrality in QCD dynamics at small x.

EIC QCD Gluon Saturation

EIC Kinematic Reconstruction

Developing machine learning model for Deep Inelastic Scattering kinematic reconstruction at the Electron-Ion Collider, including extracting x and Q² for inclusive processes and extending these techniques to additional scattering channels.

Market Microstructure Forecasting

Early-stage project: building a Python ML pipeline on limit order book data to forecast short-horizon mid-price returns and volatility. Current focus is microstructure feature design (order-flow imbalance, spread dynamics), with walk-forward validation and transaction-cost-aware backtesting planned next.
